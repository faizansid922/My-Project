OUR STORY



Moksha is India's first homegrown denim brand and one of the coolest youth apparel brands in the country. Driven to innovate, Moksha continues to experiment and push boundaries, much like today's millennials. The brand chooses to be a trendsetter rather than a fad follower and this attitude is reflected in each and every one of its products.
